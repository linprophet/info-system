### 🤝 商业大事件 / Major Deals & Strategy

**为什么本周值得看**: AI 基础模型公司集体从 API 售卖走向全栈服务——Anthropic 吸收外部资本组建合资部署公司，OpenAI 内部孵化部署企业，并同时锁定四大咨询巨头之一。这种纵向整合意味着模型层开始夺取传统系统集成商的利润池。

- [[AINews] Silicon Valley gets Serious about Services](https://www.latent.space/p/ainews-silicon-valley-gets-serious) — _Latent Space (swyx)_ · Anthropic formed an unnamed JV with Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs, raising $1.5B ($300m each) to build Claude-powered custom systems for enterprise customers, embedding Anthropic’s Applied AI staff in each engagement.
